ABOUT US
C24 was an independent platform running from 2019–2024, that explored collaboration as a creative practice. By interviewing young talents, students, or legends and featuring their projects, the platform gave an insight into the creative process. The presented projects range from design and art to music, CGI art, fashion, and motion and are therefore representative of the contemporary creative scene. C24 was Berlin-based, non-commercial, and operated with its international team worldwide. Founded in 2019.
